Victor Gou.


Creative Director

Creativity makes a happy person, we could even say it's one of the most important aspects of humankind. Look around, we have created so much. It has always been fundamental, specially now a days, right?

But here is the catch: Creativity by it self won't always have a successful outcome. It has to have a purpose behind, It has to work in conjunction with something else. So the first step on a creative process is actually to ask the right questions.

We can call this design: understanding the problems and giving creative solutions to it.

I'm Victor Goudschaal, a young creative with a purpose defined: to serve others in a creative way. By working together, asking questions, making people observe, reflect, getting them inspired in the process, and finally producing the right solutions.
